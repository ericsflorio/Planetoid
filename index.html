PLANETOID
=

celestial body larger than an asteroid, but smaller than a planet.

Every game is like a tiny planet, a unique version of reality, with its own rules and singularities.

Some are created to look as similar as possible to what we see in our planet Earth, while others try to provide experiences that we cannot have here.

All of them, however, have vocation to be discovered, deciphered, contemplated, and even subverted.

Some of these planets can even provide us with insights about our own world, and on how to improve the way we interact with it.
